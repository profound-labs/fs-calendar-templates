Forest Sangha Calendar Templates

profound-labs.github.io/fs-calendar-templates

Last updated: 2024-11-24

Calendar years included: 2026, 2027

PDF templates with calendar dates and uposatha moondays. Feel free to edit, replace or remove any part of the template pages.

Import the PDF pages into a layout- or vector graphics software such as Inkscape, Scribus, InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, etc.

Cropmarked variations are included. When you import pages without cropmarks, the page is imported already at the trimmed size. Remember to add a 3mm bleed area when preparing to print photos and graphics near the edges.

Templates

Wall trimmed page size: Choral Octavo (6.75in x 10.5in = 171.45mm x 266.7mm)

Landscape or portrait refers to the photo orientation. All the template pages are portrait (vertical).
